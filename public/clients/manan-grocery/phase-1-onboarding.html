Phase 1

Onboarding Requirements

Everything we need from Manan to get WhatsApp Business connected and verified.

🗓 Estimated:  Week 1  (parallel with OCR & Voice build)

How This Works

We handle all the technical setup. Manan just needs to provide the documents and information listed below. The WhatsApp connection itself takes 5 minutes (done in our dashboard). Meta Business Verification takes 1–10 business days depending on document quality.

📋 Business Information

Basic details about the grocery store. We need these to set up the WhatsApp Business profile.

  • Business Name (as registered) Required
    Official legal name of the grocery business (e.g., "Manan Grocery Store" or "Manan Provisions Pvt Ltd")
  • Business Address Required
    Full address including city, state, and PIN code. Must match documents.
  • Business Category Required
    We'll use "Grocery Store" / "Retail" — confirm if different.
  • Business Website or Social Media Recommended
    Website URL, Instagram page, Facebook page, or Google Maps listing. Helps Meta verify faster.
  • Business Email Required
    Ideally a domain email (e.g., manan@manangrocery.com). Gmail works too but verification may take longer.
  • Business Description (2–3 lines) Optional
    Short description for the WhatsApp Business profile. We can write this if not provided.
  • Business Logo / Profile Photo Recommended
    Square image, at least 640×640px. Used as WhatsApp profile picture.

📱 Phone Number

A phone number to use for the WhatsApp Business account.

  • Dedicated Phone Number Required
    A number that is NOT currently registered on WhatsApp (personal or business). Can be a new SIM or an existing number — but WhatsApp must be deactivated on it first. The number will receive an OTP during setup.

Important

If Manan's current WhatsApp number needs to be used, WhatsApp must be uninstalled / deregistered from that number first. We recommend getting a separate SIM for the business WhatsApp so personal use isn't affected.

👤 Facebook / Meta Account

Needed for the WhatsApp Business API signup (all WhatsApp Business accounts are managed through Meta).

  • Facebook Account Access Required
    Manan needs a Facebook account (personal is fine — it won't be public). During setup, he'll log in via a popup to authorize WhatsApp Business access. This happens in our dashboard — takes 2 minutes.
  • Meta Business Manager (we'll create if needed) Optional
    If Manan already has a Meta Business Manager account, great. If not, one gets created automatically during Embedded Signup. No action needed.

📄 Business Verification Documents

Meta requires ONE official document to verify the business identity. This unlocks higher messaging limits (250 → 1,000+ messages/day). Manan must submit this in Meta Business Manager after the initial setup.

  • Any ONE of the following documents:
Document Notes
GST Certificate Best option — fast approval. GSTIN number + registered address.
Shop Act License Municipal trade license / Gumasta license with business name.
MSME / Udyam Registration Udyam certificate with registration number.
FSSAI License Food safety license — relevant for grocery business.
Business Bank Statement Recent statement showing business name and address.
Utility Bill Electricity/water bill of the shop with business name.

Pro Tip: Use GST Certificate

GST Certificate gives the fastest Meta approval (usually 1–3 business days). The business name on the document MUST match the name used during WhatsApp signup. If Manan has a GST number, that's the best option.
