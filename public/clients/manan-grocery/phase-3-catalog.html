📦 What We Need From Manan

This is the most important data — the quality of the catalog determines the quality of the entire system.

  • Full Product Inventory Required From Manan
    Complete list of all products sold. For each product we need:
    Per product, we need:

    Product Name     e.g., "Aashirvaad Atta"
    Brand            e.g., "Aashirvaad"
    Category         e.g., "Atta & Flour"
    Size / Weight    e.g., "1kg", "5kg", "500g"
    Price (₹)       e.g., "₹90", "₹420"
    In Stock?        Yes / No
    Product Photo    (optional but recommended)
  • Product Photos Recommended From Manan
    Photos of products on shelves or individually. Doesn't need to be professional — phone camera photos are fine. These are used in the PDF catalog and WhatsApp messages. If not available, we'll use stock product images.
  • Delivery / Pickup Rules Required From Manan
    How does Manan deliver orders?
    • Home delivery? If yes, which areas/PIN codes?
    • Delivery charges? (free above ₹X, or flat rate?)
    • Minimum order amount?
    • Store pickup option?
    • Delivery time slots? (e.g., "Morning 9-12", "Evening 4-7")
  • Payment Methods Accepted Required From Manan
    Cash on delivery? UPI (Google Pay / PhonePe)? Online payment link (Razorpay)? We'll configure the order confirmation flow based on this.
  • Order Notification Preference From Manan
    How should Manan receive new orders? WhatsApp notification? Dashboard alert? SMS? Email? We'll set up the notification channel he prefers.
  • Existing Rate Card / Price List Optional From Manan
    If Manan already has a printed rate card, menu, or price list (even a photo of it), share it. We'll digitize it — saves Manan from having to type everything out.

📋 How To Share Product Data

Manan can share product data in ANY format — we'll handle the rest.

Option 1: Excel / Google Sheet (Best)

Simple spreadsheet with columns: Product Name, Brand, Category, Size, Price, In Stock. We can share a template if needed.

Option 2: Photos of Rate Card / Shelves

Just take photos of the existing rate card or product shelves. We'll use OCR to extract and digitize everything.

Option 3: Voice / WhatsApp Messages

Manan can literally send voice notes listing products and prices. We'll transcribe and organize them. Easiest option if typing isn't preferred.

Option 4: Existing PDF / Printed List

Any existing document — printed price list, supplier catalog, POS export. We'll extract and clean the data.

🔧 What AIORA Builds

Digital Catalog Database

All products stored in a structured database with categories, aliases, pricing, and stock status. Powers OCR matching, voice matching, and WhatsApp browsing.

PDF Catalog Generator

Auto-generated PDF catalog with product images, prices, and categories. Sent to customers on WhatsApp when they ask for the catalog. Regenerates automatically when products or prices change.

WhatsApp Browsing

Customers can browse by category, search by product name, and get recommendations. The AI agent understands natural language queries like "show me cooking oils under 200".

Stock Management

Simple stock in/out tracking. When products go out of stock, they're marked in the catalog and the AI tells customers immediately. Manan can update stock via the dashboard or even via WhatsApp.

Price Update System

Grocery prices change frequently. We'll build a quick-update system so Manan can update prices easily from his phone without technical hassle.

The Catalog Powers Everything

Once the catalog is loaded, all three features come together: OCR matches photos against it, Voice Notes match spoken items against it, and browsing/ordering lets customers explore it directly. One data source, three powerful channels.
