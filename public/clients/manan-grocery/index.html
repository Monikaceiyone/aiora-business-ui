Phase 2 — OCR & Voice Notes Week 1

Customers send a photo of their grocery list or a voice note — the system reads it, matches items to Manan's catalog, and creates an order automatically.

  • OCR: Photo of handwritten/printed grocery list → item extraction
  • Voice Notes: Hindi/English voice message → transcription → item extraction
  • Smart matching: fuzzy search against product catalog
  • Order summary sent back on WhatsApp for confirmation

3
Phase 3 — Product Catalog Week 2

Full product catalog management — customers can browse, search, and order from Manan's grocery inventory directly on WhatsApp.

  • Digital catalog: all products with prices, images, categories
  • WhatsApp catalog browsing (PDF / interactive list)
  • Category-wise product search ("show me dal options")
  • Cart management & order placement via chat
